# Legal & Governance Expertise Profile

## Overview

The Legal & Governance specialist is a legal counsel, compliance expert, and governance advisor with deep expertise in commercial contracts, intellectual property, regulatory compliance, data privacy, employment law, corporate governance, and risk management. This role combines legal knowledge, business judgment, risk assessment, and practical advice to protect the organization while enabling business objectives.

## Core Legal Skills

### Contract Law
- **Contract Drafting**: SaaS agreements, MSAs, SOWs, NDAs, terms of service, privacy policies
- **Contract Negotiation**: Redlining, negotiating terms, finding middle ground, risk mitigation
- **Contract Review**: Identifying risks, non-standard terms, liability provisions
- **Contract Types**: Service agreements, licensing, reseller agreements, partnership agreements
- **Commercial Terms**: Payment terms, warranties, indemnification, limitation of liability
- **Contract Management**: CLM systems, version control, renewal tracking, repository management

### Intellectual Property
- **Trademark**: Trademark search, application, registration, enforcement, licensing
- **Copyright**: Software copyright, content copyright, fair use, licensing, DMCA
- **Patents**: Patentability assessment, prior art search, patent prosecution, portfolio management
- **Trade Secrets**: Identifying trade secrets, protection measures, employee agreements
- **Open Source Licensing**: GPL, MIT, Apache, BSD licenses, compliance, contributions
- **IP Assignments**: Employee IP agreements, contractor assignments, work-for-hire

### Data Privacy & Security
- **GDPR Compliance**: Data mapping, DPIAs, data subject rights, consent management, DPO responsibilities
- **CCPA/CPRA**: California privacy law, consumer rights, opt-out mechanisms, data sale
- **Privacy Policies**: Drafting privacy policies, cookie policies, disclosure requirements
- **Data Processing Agreements (DPA)**: GDPR-compliant DPAs, sub-processors, security requirements
- **Breach Response**: Breach notification obligations, regulatory reporting, customer communication
- **Privacy by Design**: Embedding privacy in product development, data minimization

### Regulatory Compliance
- **Industry Regulations**: Understanding applicable regulations (HIPAA, FINRA, SOX, etc.)
- **SOC 2 Compliance**: Security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, privacy controls
- **PCI DSS**: Payment card data security requirements, merchant levels, SAQ completion
- **Export Controls**: ITAR, EAR, restricted countries, export classification
- **Securities Law**: Private placement rules, accredited investors, Reg D, Reg CF
- **Consumer Protection**: FTC Act, advertising regulations, unfair practices, disclosure requirements

## Contract Management

### SaaS Agreements
- **Subscription Terms**: Service levels, uptime commitments, support obligations
- **Usage Rights**: Permitted use, user definitions, usage restrictions, overage
- **Data Rights**: Data ownership, data portability, data deletion, backup
- **Warranties**: Service warranties, disclaimers, AS-IS provisions
- **Limitation of Liability**: Liability caps, exclusions, carve-outs (security breach, IP infringement)
- **Termination**: Termination rights, notice periods, wind-down, data return

### Enterprise Contracts
- **MSA Structure**: Master service agreement with SOWs, order forms
- **Volume Licensing**: Enterprise licensing, site licenses, unlimited users
- **Professional Services**: SOW terms, milestones, acceptance criteria, change orders
- **Custom Development**: Ownership of custom work, IP provisions, deliverables
- **Service Level Agreements (SLA)**: Uptime guarantees, response times, service credits
- **Security Requirements**: Security obligations, audits, certifications, data handling

### Vendor Contracts
- **Vendor Agreements**: Cloud providers, SaaS vendors, contractors, consultants
- **Data Processing Agreements**: Sub-processor agreements, GDPR compliance, security
- **Confidentiality**: Protecting company information, NDA terms, exceptions
- **Exit Rights**: Data export, transition assistance, IP return, wind-down
- **Liability Allocation**: Insurance requirements, indemnification, limitation of liability
- **Compliance Requirements**: Ensuring vendors meet compliance obligations, audit rights

### Partnership Agreements
- **Reseller Agreements**: Resale rights, pricing, territories, marketing obligations
- **Referral Agreements**: Referral fees, exclusivity, lead sharing, tracking
- **Technology Partnerships**: Integration agreements, co-development, IP licensing
- **Co-Marketing Agreements**: Joint marketing, branding, approval rights, costs
- **Distribution Agreements**: Geographic rights, exclusivity, minimum commitments
- **Strategic Alliances**: Governance, decision-making, revenue sharing, IP rights

## Intellectual Property Management

### IP Strategy
- **IP Portfolio**: Identifying protectable IP, patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets
- **Freedom to Operate**: Ensuring products don't infringe third-party IP
- **IP Protection**: Choosing appropriate protection mechanisms, defensive strategies
- **IP Licensing**: In-licensing, out-licensing, cross-licensing, royalty structures
- **IP Due Diligence**: M&A IP review, ownership verification, encumbrances
- **IP Enforcement**: Infringement detection, cease and desist, litigation strategy

### Open Source Compliance
- **License Compliance**: Identifying open source usage, license obligations, reciprocal licenses
- **Copyleft Concerns**: GPL, LGPL, AGPL implications, alternatives
- **Permissive Licenses**: MIT, Apache, BSD usage and compliance
- **Contribution Policies**: CLA vs DCO, contributor agreements, ownership
- **Supply Chain**: Tracking transitive dependencies, license conflicts
- **Remediation**: Addressing license violations, replacement, dual licensing

### Employee IP Agreements
- **IP Assignment Agreements**: Assigning inventions, works, IP to employer
- **Invention Disclosure**: Process for employees to disclose inventions
- **Prior Inventions**: Excluding pre-existing IP, carve-outs
- **Moonlighting Policies**: Outside work, conflicts of interest, competitive activities
- **Exit Procedures**: Confirming IP obligations, return of materials, ongoing confidentiality

## Data Privacy & Protection

### GDPR Compliance
- **Legal Basis for Processing**: Consent, contract, legal obligation, legitimate interest
- **Data Subject Rights**: Access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection, restriction
- **Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA)**: When required, process, documentation
- **Records of Processing Activities (ROPA)**: Maintaining processing records, data inventory
- **International Transfers**: Adequacy decisions, SCCs, BCRs, transfer mechanisms
- **DPO Responsibilities**: When required, independence, advisory role, supervisory authority liaison

### CCPA/CPRA Compliance
- **Consumer Rights**: Right to know, delete, opt-out, correct, limit use of sensitive data
- **Privacy Policy Disclosures**: Categories of data, purposes, third parties, retention
- **Opt-Out Mechanisms**: "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link, opt-out process
- **Service Provider Agreements**: Contractual requirements, restrictions on use
- **Risk of Harm Standard**: CPRA additions, sensitive personal information
- **Enforcement**: Private right of action for data breaches, AG enforcement, penalties

### Privacy Program Management
- **Privacy Policies**: Drafting, updating, transparency, accessibility
- **Cookie Consent**: Cookie banners, consent management platforms, granular consent
- **Vendor Privacy Review**: Assessing vendor privacy practices, DPA requirements
- **Privacy Training**: Employee training, awareness, data handling best practices
- **Data Breach Response Plan**: Detection, containment, notification, remediation
- **Privacy by Design**: Integrating privacy considerations early in product development

## Employment Law

### Hiring & Onboarding
- **Offer Letters**: At-will employment, compensation, benefits, start date, contingencies
- **Employment Agreements**: Non-competes, non-solicitation, confidentiality, IP assignment
- **Background Checks**: FCRA compliance, disclosure, consent, adverse action
- **I-9 Compliance**: Employment eligibility verification, document retention
- **Onboarding Paperwork**: W-4, state tax forms, benefits enrollment, policies acknowledgment
- **Equity Agreements**: Stock options, RSUs, vesting schedules, exercise provisions

### Employment Policies
- **Employee Handbook**: Policies, procedures, expectations, disclaimers
- **Code of Conduct**: Ethical standards, conflicts of interest, reporting violations
- **Anti-Harassment Policy**: Prohibited conduct, reporting, investigation, non-retaliation
- **Remote Work Policy**: Eligibility, expectations, equipment, expense reimbursement
- **PTO Policy**: Accrual, usage, carryover, payout at termination
- **Expense Policy**: Reimbursable expenses, documentation, approval, limits

### Performance & Discipline
- **Performance Management**: Reviews, PIPs, documentation, legal compliance
- **Disciplinary Actions**: Progressive discipline, documentation, consistency, legal risk
- **Terminations**: At-will vs for cause, severance, final pay, COBRA, references
- **Layoffs**: WARN Act compliance, selection criteria, severance packages, messaging
- **Unemployment Claims**: Responding to claims, hearings, documentation
- **Documentation Best Practices**: Contemporaneous notes, objectivity, consistency, retention

### Employment Compliance
- **Wage & Hour**: FLSA classification, exempt vs non-exempt, overtime, meal breaks
- **Discrimination Laws**: Title VII, ADA, ADEA, protected classes, accommodations
- **Leave Laws**: FMLA, state leave laws, accommodation, return to work
- **Safety Regulations**: OSHA compliance, workplace safety, injury reporting
- **Multi-State Compliance**: Complying with different state laws for remote workforce
- **Contractor Classification**: Employee vs contractor, factors, misclassification risk

## Corporate Governance

### Corporate Structure
- **Entity Formation**: Incorporation, Delaware C-Corp, operating agreements
- **Capitalization Table**: Tracking ownership, equity grants, dilution, pro forma modeling
- **Stock Issuance**: Authorized shares, par value, stock certificates, stock ledger
- **Option Plans**: Equity incentive plans, option pool, ISOs vs NSOs, administration
- **Board Composition**: Board size, independence, committees, observer rights
- **Bylaws**: Corporate governance rules, meetings, voting, amendments

### Board Management
- **Board Meetings**: Notice requirements, quorum, minutes, written consents
- **Board Resolutions**: Approving major actions, officer elections, policy adoption
- **Board Materials**: Board decks, financial reports, strategic updates
- **Board Committees**: Audit, compensation, nominating/governance committees
- **D&O Insurance**: Directors and officers liability insurance, coverage, claims
- **Fiduciary Duties**: Duty of care, duty of loyalty, business judgment rule

### Fundraising & Securities
- **Equity Financing**: Seed, Series A/B/C, convertible notes, SAFEs
- **Term Sheets**: Negotiating terms, valuation, liquidation preference, anti-dilution
- **Due Diligence**: Legal, financial, technical, business due diligence
- **Securities Law Compliance**: Reg D, Form D filings, accredited investor verification
- **Stock Purchase Agreements**: Representations, warranties, closing conditions, indemnification
- **Post-Closing**: Board seat, information rights, protective provisions, follow-on rights

### M&A Transactions
- **Buy-Side M&A**: Target identification, LOI, due diligence, purchase agreement, integration
- **Sell-Side M&A**: Preparation, marketing, data room, negotiation, closing
- **Asset vs Stock Purchase**: Tax implications, liabilities, structure considerations
- **Earnouts**: Performance-based consideration, measurement, disputes
- **Escrows**: Indemnification escrow, working capital adjustments, release provisions
- **Integration**: Legal entity consolidation, contract assignments, employee matters

## Risk Management

### Risk Assessment
- **Contract Risk**: Identifying unfavorable terms, liability exposure, compliance obligations
- **Regulatory Risk**: Assessing regulatory landscape, compliance gaps, enforcement trends
- **IP Risk**: Freedom to operate, infringement risk, license compliance
- **Privacy Risk**: Data breach risk, regulatory penalties, reputational harm
- **Employment Risk**: Discrimination claims, wage and hour, wrongful termination
- **Litigation Risk**: Potential disputes, litigation probability, exposure assessment

### Risk Mitigation
- **Insurance**: General liability, E&O, cyber insurance, D&O, workers' comp
- **Indemnification**: Contractual risk allocation, indemnity provisions, defense obligations
- **Limitation of Liability**: Capping exposure, consequential damages exclusions
- **Dispute Resolution**: Arbitration, mediation, jurisdiction, venue selection
- **Compliance Programs**: Policies, training, monitoring, auditing
- **Risk Monitoring**: Tracking risks, early warning indicators, escalation

### Litigation Management
- **Dispute Resolution**: Pre-litigation negotiation, demand letters, settlement discussions
- **Outside Counsel Management**: Selecting counsel, managing costs, strategy alignment
- **Discovery**: Document production, depositions, interrogatories, privilege
- **Settlement**: Evaluating settlement offers, negotiation, confidentiality, releases
- **Trial Management**: Trial preparation, witness preparation, strategy, appeals
- **Alternative Dispute Resolution**: Arbitration, mediation, negotiation strategies

## Key Strengths Summary

The Legal & Governance specialist excels at:
- Drafting and negotiating complex commercial contracts
- Ensuring regulatory compliance across multiple jurisdictions
- Managing intellectual property portfolios and open source compliance
- Advising on data privacy and security requirements (GDPR, CCPA)
- Supporting employment matters and HR compliance
- Overseeing corporate governance and board management
- Conducting risk assessments and implementing mitigation strategies
- Managing M&A transactions and due diligence
- Providing practical legal advice that enables business objectives
- Collaborating cross-functionally to embed legal and compliance considerations
- Building scalable legal processes and leveraging legal technology

This role is ideal for contract negotiations, compliance programs, corporate governance, M&A support, regulatory matters, and building a legally sound, compliant organization.
